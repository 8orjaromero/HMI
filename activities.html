Walking tour through the parks and hideen places of San Sebastian

We will start in the young Egia neighborhood, and shortly after we will head to Cristina Enea, a hidden gem just15 minutes from the city center. Along this park we will discover a palace that houses a story of love and peculiaranimals, then we will cross the river and the main residential neighborhood of the city, Amara. There, you can explore the main aspects of the daily life of modern Basque society, and then head to Parque Aiete, which is oneof the largest in the city, but probably one of the least known. There, I will show you huge trees, a hidden cave and a palace where the most loved and hated personalities of this country stayed during their vacations.Finally we will descend to the last park and palace, Miramar, where a Spanish queen put San Sebastián on the map duringthe "belle époque" for years to come. Finally, we will arrive at a local bar to enjoy all the lush greenery and intriguinghistory with a well-deserved hot or cold drink.

Price:10€

Hiking tour to Mount Ernio from San Sebastián

Escape the busy life of the city and head to the mountains for a day trip in nature to this iconic Basque mountain. We will head towards the base of Mount Ernio to begin the hike to the top. During the trail we will have the opportunity to admire the beautiful landscape of the Basque countryside. On the way downhill from the top,we'll stop at a local farm for a well-deserved snack, before heading back to San Sebastian. Hotel pickup and drop-off included.

Price:18€
