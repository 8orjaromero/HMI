Akelarre

Since 1970, Pedro Subijana develops a personal cuisine, and the Akelarre restaurant has turned into a classic of Basque cuisine. Located in the Igeldo district in San Sebastian, it offers spectacular views of the Cantabrian coast from its large windows. We can enjoy excellent desserts and impeccable service in its three simply and elegantly decorated dining rooms.

Arzak

The house hosting this internationally-acclaimed restaurant was built in 1897 in order to function as cellar and inn. Later on it became a food house specialising in banquets. In 1966 Juan Mari Arzak took control of the restaurant and elevated it to the culinary top. Nowadays his daughter Elena helps him. The menu reflects the permanent creation of new dishes with constant and traditional roots. The restaurant consists of a "laboratory" that analyses flavours, textures and production-processes. Efficient and personalised service. Spectacular wine list with the best denomination of origin wines.
